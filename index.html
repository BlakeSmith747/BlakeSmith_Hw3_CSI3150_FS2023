Is a trip to New York right for you?

New York City, the most populus city in the United States, it is a land known as the concrete jungle where dreams are made of. Known for sprawling urban landscape and the subway's of mass public transportation. From Broadway to the Statue of Liberty come check out what NYC has to offer. NY style pizza to dirty water dogs, New York offers unparalled culinary experience. Bagels, pizza, authentic asian food markets and amazing pasta New York offers a diverse range of experiences. Stop by the scaffolding and see renovations and updates as the various construction workers silently wish you would move away so they can complete their work. Visitors can witness the spectacular queues of tourists taking photos with painted people and pets. Witness New York City's rich culture be overshadowed by tourist traps such as Times Square and of course you have to visit the iconic Central Park. Whether you like exploring or simply sitting in silence New York will broaden your horizons in all new ways.

New York's dense urban terrain is home to the Metro Transit Authority, where the transportation is affordable but not always clean.
